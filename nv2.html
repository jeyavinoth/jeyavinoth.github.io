What is an Escape Room ?

Escape room adventures is one where a team of players are locked in a room. The team has to find clues, solve puzzles, play games, to find a way out of the room within the allocated time (usually 60 minutes). This is a thrill seeking, fun adventure ride. You have to use your smarts to unlock keys, test your hand-eye cordination in beating games. Don't worry, there is always an emergency exit to each room.

Who is the entertainment for ?

Escape rooms are an entertainment for all age groups. We have multiple themed rooms, that can suit a large variety of players. Want a more high-tech room, try out our System Error room. Want a more Flint, Detroit or Michigan themed room, try out our Michigan Murder room. This could be a family entertainment or an activity for a group of friends. All alone, no problem, come join another group and meet new and exciting people with similar tastes. Want a team building experience for your coperate team, we are ideal for that as well.
